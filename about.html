ASTK Story

What you wear should reflect who you are, and ASTK is a casual wear shop that offers a wide variety of men and women’s clothing to help you better express the real YOU.
From loungewear to active wear and cotton workout clothes, ASTK wears don’t just provide everyday attires; we offer comfy wears with the latest fashion trends that add just the right amount of cheer to your wardrobe!
ASTK fashion is always on fleek, always current, and ready to deliver that contemporary look to everyone. We even take care of the smallest details of your outfit with the coolest socks for men and socks for women.
Shop the latest affordable street wear and casual outfits from ASTK’s men and women’s clothing online shop, all without breaking the bank! Stick around for the ride, it’s time to live your best life.

Our Core

ASTK signifies the ‘free spirit’ in you. We are driven by passion that leads to success. Our brand is human-centric, driven by the people and towards them, creating a balance of practical and personal relations.

Our Value

The softness of our fabrics, the relaxing texture and the sleek design define the value of ASTK. Based on the deepest research, we’ve submitted an utter quality that suits your body dynamically and your fashion taste. Every aspect is crafted with specialty including the colors, fabrics, designs..etc.

Our Vision

To inspire confidence that you pull off every day, all while being comfortable and cozy all day long. ASTK aims to keep providing total customer satisfaction. So you become unstoppable, outspoken, and stand out.

Our Mission

We strive to enhance the value-for-money experience when it comes to men and women’s fashion. ASTK store provides you with the essential casual wear outfits and a mission to impress.

Our Product
